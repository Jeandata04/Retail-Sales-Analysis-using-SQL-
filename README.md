## Retail-Sales-Analysis-using-SQL-
This project analyzes a retail sales dataset using SQL to uncover key business insights related to revenue performance, customer behavior, product profitability, and shipping efficiency.

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains transactional sales records including:
Order ID
Order Date
Ship Date
Customer Name
Customer ID
State
Product ID
Product Name
Category
Sales 

## Business  Questions
1. What is the total revenue generated?
2. What are the monthly sales trends?
3. Who are the top 5 customers by revenue?
4. Which product categories generate the highest profit?
5. How long does shipping take on average?
6. Which state experiences the longest shipping delays?

## SQL Concepts Used
Aggregations (SUM, AVG, COUNT)
GROUP BY
ORDER BY
LIMIT
WHERE
Date functions (DATEDIFF)
Data segmentation

## Key findings 
1. The company experienced significant shipping delays concentrated in 3 major regions(Washington, Kentucky, Nebraska)
2. Furniture, Technology and Office supplies generate the most profit to the company
3. There are 5 top customers who outperform others in generating the company's profitability with Brosina Hoffman being in the lead
4. The monthly sales are decreasing significantly throughout different months and year, although there are different peak seasons.
5. Shipping takes an average of 10 days
